Early Life

  • Albert Einstein was born in 1879 in Ulm, Germany
  • He was the first child born to Hermann and Pauline Einstein
  • By the age of twelve he had taught himself geometry
  • In 1900 he graduated as a teacher of math and physics
  • In 1901 he took a job as a temporary high school teacher and married Mileva Maritsch.
  • He earned a Ph.D. in 1905.

Quantum Theory of Light

Einstein’s quantum theory of light proposed that light is composed of small packets of energy called photons that have wave-like properties. In this theory he also explained the emission of electrons from some metals they’re struck by lightning – this was called the photoelectric effect.

E=mc2


He demonstrated the link between mass and energy that led to the nuclear energy today. (Energy = mass times the speed of light squared)
